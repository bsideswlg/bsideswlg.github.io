Venue & Logistics

Shed 6, 4 Queens Wharf, Te Aro, Wellington 6140

23 & 24th of November 2017

Shed 6 is in the middle of town, near the train station, and close to many cafes, pubs, and hotels. It will be broken into multiple talk/workshop areas, consisting of a large talk track, a smaller talk/workshop track, and a third room for other events.

Schedule of Events

BSides Wellington will take place with two tracks, one in each room, and a breakout room for people to chill in. Main events such as keynotes and plenary speakers will take place entirely in Room One.
