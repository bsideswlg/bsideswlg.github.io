BSides Wellington will take place on the 23rd and 24th of November 2017

Location & Logistics

BSides Wellington will take place in Shed 6 on Queens Wharf. Shed6 is in the middle of town, near the train station, and close to many cafes, pubs, and hotels.

For BSides Wellington, Shed 6 will be broken into multiple talk/workshop areas, consisting of a large talk track, a smaller talk/workshop track, and a third room for other events.
