Welcome to BSides Wellington

BSidesWLG 2017 is a diverse and inclusive information security conference welcoming anyone and everyone that is interested in security. BSides is an open platform that provides a rare opportunity to share experiences, ideas, insights, and develop longstanding relationships with others in the security community.

Our 2017 conference will provide approximately 450 participants with the choice of 2 tracks that will cover a wide range of information security disciplines and topics, with a workshop space, a CTF competition, and much more!

BSidesWLG 2017 will be held on the 23 and 24 November 2017 at Shed6 in Wellington.

Our Call For Papers is open until August 26th! Go Submit!
