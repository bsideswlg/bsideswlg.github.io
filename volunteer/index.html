Volunteer

Interested in helping at BSidesWLG 2017?

As a community-driven event, BSidesWLG would not be possible without the work of volunteers. The organisers are working hard to build a fun and exciting agenda. In order to execute this event and make it successful, we seeking volunteers to help with tasks before and on the two days of the event (November 23rd-24th, 2017), which include, but are not limited to:

  • Registration / Information
  • Merchandise desk
  • Audio/Visual
  • Room moderators / security
  • Speaker wrangling
  • Top secret undercover missions

We understand that our volunteers want to hear some of the talks as well, and we will work with you to ensure you are able to do so.

You can sign up to be a volunteer here. Email info@bsides.nz if you have questions.

Also, join us on Slack for real-time updates
